SAM Multi-Cat Extension Summary
===============================

Both-cat images available: 12
Solo Tig images used for crop classifier: 30
Solo Buster images used for crop classifier: 30
Crop feature extractor: ResNet18 pretrained on ImageNet
Crop classifier: Logistic regression
Mask generator: Segment Anything Model, ViT-B
Post-processing: non-max suppression on SAM masks and classified crop boxes

Interpretation note:
This extension is qualitative rather than a formal detection benchmark. The project does not include hand-labeled bounding boxes, so the outputs are inspected visually rather than scored with object-detection metrics.
